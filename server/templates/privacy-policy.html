Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026 | Version 1.0.0

Summary: StaffHub collects only the data necessary to provide staff management services. We do not sell your personal information. You have full control over your data under GDPR and Kosovo data protection laws.

1. Introduction

StaffHub ("we", "our", or "us") operates the StaffHub mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

This policy is governed by the laws of the Republic of Kosovo and complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of the European Union, Law No. 06/L-082 on Protection of Personal Data of Kosovo, and applicable international data protection standards.

2. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

  • Company: StaffHub Solutions
  • Address: Pristina, Republic of Kosovo
  • Email: privacy@staffhub.app

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile photo
  • Employment Data: Job title, role, department, work location, employment contract details
  • Work Records: Schedules, shifts, time entries, break times
  • Communications: Requests, reports, messages sent through the App
  • Financial Information: Salary data, payment records (for authorized roles only)

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent, interaction patterns
  • Log Data: Access times, error logs, performance data

3.3 Location Data

We may collect location data only when you use the SOS emergency feature, and only with your explicit consent. Location data is used solely to assist emergency services.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's staff management functionality
  • To manage work schedules, shifts, and time tracking
  • To process requests, reports, and internal communications
  • To calculate and display salary information (for authorized users)
  • To facilitate emergency SOS alerts
  • To send important notifications about your employment
  • To improve and optimize the App
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR and Kosovo data protection law, we process your data based on:

  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary for your employment relationship
  • Legitimate Interests: Business operations, security, and App improvement
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with employment and tax laws
  • Consent: For optional features like location services and marketing

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Your Employer: Company administrators and managers have access to work-related data
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, and support services (with data processing agreements)
  • Emergency Services: When you activate SOS features
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect rights and safety

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for:

  • Active Employment: Throughout your employment with your company
  • Post-Employment: As required by Kosovo labor law (typically 5 years for employment records)
  • Financial Records: As required by tax law (typically 10 years)

You may request earlier deletion subject to legal requirements.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR and Kosovo law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@staffhub.app

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication with password hashing
  • Role-based access controls
  • Regular security audits
  • Employee data protection training

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Kosovo. We ensure appropriate safeguards through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions for recipient countries
  • Binding corporate rules where applicable

11. Children's Privacy

StaffHub is not intended for individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact and Complaints

For privacy inquiries or complaints:

  • Email: privacy@staffhub.app
  • Data Protection Authority: You may lodge a complaint with the Information and Privacy Agency of Kosovo or your local supervisory authority

14. Emergency SOS Disclaimer

The SOS feature in StaffHub is provided as a convenience tool only. It is not a substitute for official emergency services (112 in Kosovo/Europe). In any emergency, always contact official emergency services directly. We are not responsible for any delays, failures, or outcomes related to the SOS feature.

Questions? Contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@staffhub.app
