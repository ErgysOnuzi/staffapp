## Full Description (4000 characters max)

StaffHub is your all-in-one staff management solution designed for businesses of all sizes. From scheduling shifts to managing requests and tracking salary information, StaffHub streamlines your workforce operations.

**For Employees:**
- View your work schedule at a glance with our intuitive weekly calendar
- Submit time-off requests and reports directly from your phone
- Track your accumulated salary and view payment history
- Access emergency SOS features for workplace safety
- Receive important notifications about schedule changes

**For Managers & Supervisors:**
- View your entire team's status in real-time
- Approve or reject staff requests with one tap
- See who's working today and their current shifts
- Manage team schedules efficiently

**For Administrators & HR:**
- Complete user management - add, edit, and manage all employees
- View company-wide statistics and reports
- Manage multiple locations/markets
- Full access to all staff data and analytics

**Key Features:**
- Role-based access control with 7 user levels
- Secure login with company code authentication
- Real-time schedule updates
- Request and report submission system
- Salary tracking and payment history
- Emergency SOS with quick access to services
- Multi-language support
- Dark and light theme options
- GDPR compliant data handling

**Built for Kosovo and Beyond:**
StaffHub is designed with Kosovo labor law compliance in mind while meeting GDPR requirements for data protection. Perfect for businesses operating in Kosovo and the European Union.

**Security First:**
Your data is protected with industry-standard encryption. All communications are secured with HTTPS, and we never share your personal information with third parties.

Download StaffHub today and transform how you manage your workforce!
