Graphics
Manage your app icon, screenshots and videos to promote your app on Google Play. Review the content guidelines before uploading new graphics. If you add translations for your Store Listing without localised graphics, we will use the graphics from your default language.

App icon *
Upload an icon
Your app icon must be a PNG or JPEG, up to 1 MB, 512 px by 512 px, and meet our design specifications and metadata policy

Feature graphic *
Upload a feature graphic
Your feature graphic must be a PNG or JPEG, up to 15 MB and 1,024 px by 500 px

Video
