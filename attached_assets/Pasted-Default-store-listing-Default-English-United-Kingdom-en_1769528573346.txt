Full description *
Staff App is a business management application designed to help companies manage their employees, contracts and internal requests in one place.
The app provides role-based access for staff, managers and administrators.
Main features:
• Secure login with role-based permissions
• Employee profile management
• Contract start and end date tracking
• Internal request management
• Admin dashboard for full system control
• Manager access to assigned staff
• Staff access to personal data only
Staff App is built for internal company use and is suitable for small and medium-sized businesses that want a simple and secure way to manage their workforce.
The app does not include advertisements and does not sell user data.
